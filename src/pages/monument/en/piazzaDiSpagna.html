Piazza Di Spagna

Piazza di Spagna, the Roman living room at the foot of the famous Spanish Steps, has enchanted for centuries with its timeless elegance

History and construction

The Barcaccia Fountain was built between 1627 and 1629 during the papacy of Urban VIII Barberini. It was designed by Pietro Bernini and completed with the help of his son, Gian Lorenzo Bernini. The fountain was commissioned to improve the water supply in Rome, utilizing water from the Acquedotto Vergine. Its unique shape, resembling a half-sunken boat, was inspired by a historical event: during the flood of 1598, a boat was said to have been stranded in the square when the Tiber River overflowed.

The fountain was constructed from travertine stone and features the papal coat of arms with sun emblems, a symbol of the Barberini family.

How it is used

Today, the fountain is mainly used as a tourist attraction and as a source of fresh water. However, it has also been the site of protests and football clashes.

Recent facts

The fountain was restored in 2014 to clean and preserve its structure. However, in 2015, it was damaged by Dutch football fans, causing severe scratches and cracks.The fountain has been featured in many movies, including Roman Holiday, with Audrey Hepburn and Gregory Peck.

Legends and fun facts

Unlike most fountains, the Barcaccia does not have high water jets because of the low water pressure from the Acquedotto Vergine. For this reason, the fountain is not very large. The strange shape of the boat is thought to be inspired by a "Naumachia," a type of boat used mainly for naval combat. The fountain has been a meeting point for Romans and tourists for centuries. Piazza di Spagna also has a mysterious legend. On autumn nights, the ghost of a woman in a black veil is said to appear. She walks through Trastevere, crosses Ponte Garibaldi, and reaches the Barcaccia fountain, where she suddenly disappears when a voice shouts, "Lorenza!" People say she is Lorenza Feliciani, the wife of Cagliostro, a magician and alchemist. He was allegedly arrested here after she accused him of witchcraft.
